Chapter One: Haunted Village
So, this story is based on true events there is a village called kheri rao wali. everyone says that the village is constructed on a graveyard. it was really terryfying. this thing was happend on 15th july 1938.9 years before our independence. About Characters Friends of Harry- john, charles, shabina and rohan there was a person called harry. He was a writer. he was studying in a college one day he decided to go on a trip with his friends he thought that he will write about his journey then he talked to his friends and they said yes after that they decided to go to himanchal pradesh and route was going from delhi,haryana and punjab he thought that he will capture the photos of beautiful fields and he will go from forests and he will see some wild animals. they booked a travel bus for going to himanchal pradesh they started moving from jaipur when they reached delhi they ate many tasty things after that they started moving forward to haryana when they reached in the center of haryana they reached to a place called kaithal when they moved little bit forward they reached to a village and then the night has come so, they thought that they will stay at village when they entered in a house the house was full of gold and there was no one in the village so, they decided to stay there so they slept at night. -Manpreet Singh
